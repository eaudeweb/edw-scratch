United Nations Environment Programme
2014/DTIE/SCP/001
18-Jun-2014
16-Jul-2014 17:00
(GMT +1.00) Brussels, Copenhagen, Madrid, Paris

The training simulator includes a software, using a Microsoft Windows-based (latest operational version) simulation environment, operation system and IT-hardware, including instructor and training stations, for a Pressurized Water Reactor type nuclear power plant, approximately 1000/1200 MWe. The real-time computer system multifunction Simulator is required to reach the educational and training objectives:

  • Initial training to understand physical and thermo hydraulic processes and normal and abnormal nuclear plant operating conditions
  • Acquiring practical skills on control of the plant under normal and abnormal operating conditions.

The simulator (software and hardware product) shall be developed, supplied and installed at the Vietnam Atomic Energy Agency.
